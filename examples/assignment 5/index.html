    Bahamas is home to a large array of aquatic life. It is a beautiful tourist destination for people around the world. From conch fritters to sea bass, the menus are delicious all over the island. There are also numerous activities to enjoy such as snorkeling and fishing.

    • Nassau
    • Harbour Island
    • Atlantis

    Nassau is the capital of the Bahamas. It is a popular cruise destination with mumerous shops, restaurants, museums, and colorful colonial buildings.

    Harbour Island is a beautiful location with pink sand beaches. One can go snorkeling, diving, and fishing. Golf carts are present all ovet this island.

    Atlantis is a lovely resort with incredible architecture. It mimics the look of the sunken city and even has an aquarium inside. There are a lot of water slides to enjoy.
