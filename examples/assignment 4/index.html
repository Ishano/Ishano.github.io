HOW TO DRAW

You can learn the process of drawing comic book characters on this page. No previous drawing experience is required. Feel free to explore and enjoy!

  1. Step1: Pencil

    First, one must sketch the character first with a pencil. It can be rough and imperfect during this stage.

  2. Step2: Ink

    Secondly, one must ink the sketch with a micron pen. They must outline the pencils with black ink for the details the pop out. Hatching and shading give the character a sense of depth.

  3. Step3: Color

    Lastly, one must color the drawing in for it to look visually appealing. It can be done with color pencils or paint. One should try to use complementary colors to create a balanced drawing.
