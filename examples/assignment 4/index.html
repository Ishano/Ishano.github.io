  1. Step1: Pencil

    First, one must sketch the character first with a pencil. It can be rough and imperfect during this stage.

  2. Step2: Ink

    Secondly, one must ink the sketch with a micron pen. They must outline the pencils with black ink for the details the pop out.

  3. Step3: Color

    Lastly, one must color the drawing in for it to look visually appealing. It can be done with color pencils or paint.
